Shiba Inu

The Shiba Inu is the smallest of the six original and distinct spitz breeds of dog from Japan. A small, agile dog that copes very well with mountainous terrain, the Shiba Inu was originally bred for hunting.

Birthday Parties

Looking for a cool and fun place to have your child's birthday party? Give that special child a truly unique day at Big Joel's Safari!

What's included in our Safari Birthday Package

  • Admission for 10 kids and 10 adults to Big Joel's Safari.
  • Additional guests must pay the standard park entree fee.
  • Free admission for the birthday child's parents and up to four Grandparents.
  • Each kids will recieve a small souvenir feed cup with animal feed for the animals.
  • Party guests have private access to the Safari Party Room for 2 hours, with 30 additional minutes for prep time beforehand.
  • The party room has Safari themed decorations and is climate controlled.
  • Basic cups, plates, napkins, and utensils are provided.

Party Rules

We encourage our guests go over the rules before their party.

  • All guests must check in at the front desk before entering the park.
  • Guests are permitted to bring food and drink into the party room. Alchohol is prohibited.
  • All food and drink must stay in the party room for the safety of our animals.
  • Guests are permitted to bring balloons and other party decorations.
  • Balloons and party favors, such as whistles are to stay in the party room.
