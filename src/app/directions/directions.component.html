Best age to visit? Animal lovers of all ages are welcome. Is the park wheelchair accessible? The park is partially wheelchair accessible. There are no paved walkways with only natural and gravel terrain. Which animals can be touched and fed? Almost all animals that can walk up to the fence are approachable, unless signs say otherwise. Some love to be touched, but cannot be fed. Some love to be fed, but not touched. The animals can be fed by hand, spoon, or special feed tubes. Feeding directly from the feed cup is discouraged. Hand washing stations are near the front of the park. Are reservations required for large groups? School tours and field trips must be scheduled in advance.
