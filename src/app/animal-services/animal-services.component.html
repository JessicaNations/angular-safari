Live Nativities & Mobile Animal Services!

  • Big Joel's Safari provides Live Nativities during the holiday season.
  • Want to add something special to your nativity scene, church program, or holiday event this season?
  • We have provided animals for many churches and organizations.
  • We generally provide traditional barn animals such as donkeys, goats, sheep, small cow, and sometimes poultry on request. We can of course provide a camel ( or camels ) which are often the highlight of the show.
  • All live nativities are customized to suit your needs, so we do not have general pricing for this service.
  • For a price quote and availability you can call the park office 636-745-3031.
  • So we can give you a better quote please know the date or dates, how long your event will be, location, & any special scenarios.
